About

Building at the edge of AI & ML

I'm a senior at UC Berkeley studying Data Science with a Robotics domain emphasis, focused on becoming a production ML Engineer. I work at the intersection of rigorous mathematics, large language models, and real software — shipping systems that ingest messy real-world data, reason over it, and deliver useful outputs. My projects span autonomous agentic AI, spatial ML for social equity, and full-stack financial simulation.

ML & AI focus

LLM engineering, RAG pipelines, agentic multi-agent workflows, deep learning with PyTorch and TensorFlow, and Monte Carlo simulation. I care about systems that work end-to-end in production.

Engineering philosophy

Strong math background (A.S. Mathematics, A.S. Physics) means I derive models from first principles, not just import libraries. I want to own everything from architecture to deployment and monitoring.

Research interests

Multi-agent AI systems, LLM reasoning at scale, retrieval-augmented generation, spatial ML for equity analysis, and stochastic simulation. Contributing to UC Berkeley open projects.
